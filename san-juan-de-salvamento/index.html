San Juan de Salvamento

November 7, 2022

This is a poem which I found written on a lamp post in the town of Ushuaia, Argentina.

Faro
ojo avizor faro
leyenda faro, luz
nuevos tiempos
dejan en tu orilla
la tormenta
tormento
